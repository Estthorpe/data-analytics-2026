## 🏆 Project 01 Highlights — Retail Banking Churn

> Full case study: [`project-01-finance-retail-churn/`](project-01-finance-retail-churn/)

- **20.37% churn rate** identified across 10,000 customer records
- **Germany churns at 2×** the France/Spain rate — £97.9M balance at risk
- **4,916 customers** classified as Intervene Now by predictive model
- **£142.5M** in combined account value at risk
- **347:1 ROI** projected from targeted retention programme
- **AUC-ROC 0.853** — Random Forest outperforms Logistic Regression baseline
